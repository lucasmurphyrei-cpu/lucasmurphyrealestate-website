## SEO & AI Discoverability
- Semantic HTML with proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
- Schema.org structured data (LocalBusiness, RealEstateAgent) via JSON-LD
- Meta titles and descriptions targeting "real estate Milwaukee County," "Waukesha County realtor," etc.
- Internal linking between all guide and resource pages for AI crawlability
- Clean URL structure (e.g., `/guides/first-time-home-buyers`, `/resources/lenders`)
- Open Graph and Twitter meta tags for social sharing
- Sitemap-ready architecture
